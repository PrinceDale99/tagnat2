A Message from the Principal

On behalf of the entire faculty and staff of Taguig National High School, I extend a 
heartfelt welcome to our website! Whether you are a prospective student, a returning learner, a dedicated 
parent, or a valued member of the community, we are thrilled to have you here.

excellence and a strong commitment to nurturing the well-being of our students. We 
are dedicated to providing a stimulating and supportive learning environment that 
empowers students to reach their full potential.
            
Our website serves as a valuable resource for anyone interested in learning more 
about our school. Here, you will find information about our academic programs,

encourage you to explore the various sections and discover all that Taguig National 
High School has to offer.

If you have any questions or require further information, please do not hesitate to contact 
our school administration. We are here to guide you and support you on your educational journey.
            
Welcome once again to Taguig National High School! We look forward to being a part of your 
success story.
            
Sincerely,

Enrollment Requirements and Process for Taguig National High School (TNHS)



Since specific enrollment details might change depending on the school year, it's best to get the latest information
directly from Taguig National High School. Here's how you can approach this:


1. Contact TNHS:
Call the school at (phone number unavailable). You can also try sending an email to inquire about enrollment. Unfortunately, the school
email address isn't publicly available online.


2. Visit the School:
Head to Taguig National High School located at IRM Road corner A. Reyes Street, New Lower Bicutan, Taguig City. Talk to the registrar's office for enrollment inquiries and requirements.


General Enrollment Requirements (These might vary, so confirm with TNHS):
• Report Card (from previous school)
• Birth Certificate (Certified True Copy)
• Good Conduct Certificate (from previous school)
• Medical Certificate
• Recent passport-sized ID picture
• Barangay Certification


Enrollment Process (This might vary, so confirm with TNHS):
• Secure enrollment forms from the registrar's office.
• Fill out the forms completely.
• Submit the accomplished forms along with the required documents.
• Pay enrollment fees (if any).
• Attend the designated schedule for new students (orientation, etc.)


Additional Tips:
• Inquire about the enrollment schedule or period.
• Prepare all the requirements beforehand to avoid delays
. • Be sure to clarify if there are any specific requirements for transferees or students with special needs.


By following these steps and contacting TNHS directly, you should get a clear
understanding of the enrollment process and requirements for the upcoming school year.

Q: What language is used for instruction at TNHS?

A: Filipino (Tagalog) is the primary language used. English is used in English classes and designated "English Speaking Zones". Arabic is used for Madrasa classes, and other languages might be spoken by students informally. French and Portuguese are offered as optional languages.
